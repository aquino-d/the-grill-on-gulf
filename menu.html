Dinner

Leave the grilling to us. We offer a wide variety of fish selections from around the globe. From Ahi Tuna and Chilean Sea Bass to Rainbow Trout and Salmon, our market-fresh fish are cooked to perfection over a wood-burning grill that gives it that unique, The Grill on Gulf flavor. Top it off with a fresh-grilled lemon or one of our complementary Signature Sauces and dinner is complete.
